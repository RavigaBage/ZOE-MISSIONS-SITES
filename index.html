“Go therefore and make disciples of all nations.”

Zoe Missions exists to carry the light of Christ into every community and nation. Our mission is to reach the unreached, serve the needy, and nurture spiritual growth through the power of the Gospel. Who are we ?

Winning Souls
and Advancing the Kingdom Across the Nations

At Zoe Missions, we are a Christ-centered church dedicated to spreading the Gospel and serving those in need — both locally and globally. Our heart beats for missions, compassion, and transformation. We also place a strong focus on children’s ministry, nurturing young believers through engaging, age-appropriate programs that help them grow in faith. Beyond ministry, we invest in education and spiritual development, equipping every member to serve God and impact their world.

Our Mission in Action

See How Lives Are Being Transformed Across the Nations

community impact

Lasting Impact
and Transforming lives

Over the past 10 years of active missions, Zoe has witnessed the faithful hand of God at work. Through relentless outreach and dedication, we have established nine local churches across various communities — each one a beacon of hope and a center for spreading the Gospel. These local assemblies now serve as vibrant hubs of worship, discipleship, and community transformation, continuing the mission to win souls and build the Kingdom of God.

medicals service

Caring for Bodies
and Ministering to Souls in the Name of Jesus

As part of our outreach efforts, Zoe Missions provides essential medical services during our missions. We believe that sharing the love of Christ goes beyond words — it is demonstrated through acts of care and compassion. Our medical team offers free health screenings, treatments, and medication to individuals and families in underserved communities, bringing both physical healing and the hope of the Gospel to those we serve.

Donations

Extending a Helping Hand
and Reflecting God’s Heart to the Needy

At Zoe Missions, we believe that love is most powerful when it is shown through action. As part of our outreach, we donate food, clothing, and essential supplies to families and communities in need. Each act of giving reflects the heart of Christ — meeting physical needs while opening doors for the message of hope. Through these gestures of compassion, lives are restored, hearts are encouraged, and the light of the Gospel continues to shine.
