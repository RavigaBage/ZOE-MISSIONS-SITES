The Missionary Legacy of Pastor Emmanuel Agordorku (2015–2025)

The Vision

In 2015, Pastor Emmanuel Agordorku led a missionary drive that would transform the spiritual landscape of the Nyanyano/Kasoa Area. Marking ten solid years of establishing churches, his ministry became a steady light across rural communities nationwide.
