For Believers & Seekers

I need spiritual help now – what do I do?

If you are struggling with doubt, seeking meaning, or facing a spiritual crisis, call on the name of Jesus Christ and pray. Confess your need for Him and His guidance.

Find a reliable Christian community (church) where you can receive immediate support, prayer, and discipleship. The Bible says: “Draw near to God, and he will draw near to you.” (James 4:8).

Seek out a trusted church leader, pastor, or mentor for counsel and accountability. They can provide guidance rooted in scripture and connect you with ongoing support...

Here are some ways to strengthen your walk with God:

  • Reading the Holy Bible daily for wisdom and direction
  • Committing to consistent prayer and communion with God
  • Participating in fellowship with other believers

The Bible warns us to be vigilant against false teachings and spiritual dangers that draw us away from the truth of the Gospel. Test all spirits according to scripture.

If you suspect something is inconsistent with the Bible:

  1. Consult Scripture: Read the relevant passage in context.
  2. Seek Council: Talk to a trusted, biblically sound leader.
  3. Pray: Ask the Holy Spirit for discernment and wisdom.
  4. Flee Falsehood: Reject anything that contradicts the deity of Christ or salvation by grace.

The main signs of healthy teaching include: Exaltation of Christ, adherence to the Bible, and fruit of the Spirit.
