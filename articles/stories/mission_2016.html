A Multi-Faceted Outreach

The 2016 mission to Tomefa was characterized by its diversity in approach. Recognizing that every heart responds differently, the team employed a mix of public proclamation and private dialogue.

Public Crusades One-on-One Counseling Door-to-Door Community Support

The Power of Counseling

While the crusades brought the community together, it was the counseling sessions that allowed for deep, personal breakthroughs. By sitting down with individuals and families, the mission team was able to address specific spiritual needs and life challenges.

This "heart-to-heart" strategy ensured that those who came forward were given the guidance needed to begin their new journey with clarity and support.
