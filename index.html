Mission in Action

How Lives Are Being Transformed

See how the Gospel moves through word, deed, and compassion across our communities.

Evangelism

Encircling the Globe with the Gospel

Over 10 years, Zoe in collaboration with Tirzah Oasis Foundation has brought Christ's message to communities, resulting in salvation, healing encounters, and baptisms.

Medical Outreach

Caring for Bodies, Healing Hearts

Our medical team provides free health screenings, treatments, and medication to families in underserved communities — love made tangible.

Community Service

Extending a Hand to Those in Need

We donate food, clothing, and essential supplies — meeting physical needs while opening doors for the message of hope and restoration.

Our Vision

To see lives transformed, communities impacted, and generations empowered through the preaching of the Gospel — building a vibrant, Spirit-filled community that honours God and reflects His love.

— Zoe Tirzah Mission Statement
