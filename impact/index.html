🌍 A Decade of Global Transformation

10+ Years of Mission Impact

For more than a decade, our missions organization has carried the message of hope, compassion, and transformation into communities across continents. Through evangelism, humanitarian outreach, education support, medical missions, and leadership development, thousands of lives have been empowered spiritually and socially.

What began as a small outreach movement has grown into a global mission network reaching vulnerable families, underserved communities, and emerging church leaders. From remote villages to urban centers, every mission trip, relief campaign, and discipleship initiative has contributed to lasting change and community restoration.

Today, our impact continues to expand through strategic partnerships, youth mobilization, church planting initiatives, and sustainable community projects designed to transform generations for years to come.

A Decade of Mission Impact

Communities Reached • Souls Won • Lives Transformed

Over the past decade, the Zoe-Tirzah Missions movement has carried the Gospel into towns, villages, and underserved communities across Ghana. Through crusades, house-to-house evangelism, prayer outreach, counseling sessions, and community engagement, thousands of lives have encountered hope, healing, and spiritual transformation.

1. Domeabra • 2015
Historic Mission Breakthrough

Revival Ignites Transformation in Domeabra

250 Souls Won Through Evangelism & Community Outreach

The Domeabra mission became one of the ministry’s most remarkable early breakthroughs. Through crusades, prayer sessions, and personal evangelism, over 250 individuals surrendered their lives to Christ. A defining moment came when the community’s chief priestess and her assistant publicly renounced idol worship and joined the church community.
