The Great Harvest

In 2015, the Domeabra community witnessed a spiritual shift that would change its landscape forever. Through dedicated outreach and fervent prayer, the mission team reached into the heart of the village.

The message of hope resonated deeply, leading to a total of 250 souls accepting the call to faith during the week-long crusade.
