Our Community Impact

Here’s a window into the places we’ve touched, the lives uplifted, and the beautiful ripple of kindness made possible by generous hearts. Every project shown here is a true story of partnership, grit, and hope.

How the Funds Were Raised

It began with small steps. A youth group decided to donate a portion of their monthly offerings. Then local families joined, giving what they could. A few partners abroad added support. Slowly the river grew from drops into streams. Every cedi raised was tracked, documented, and channeled directly into community needs like clean water, school support, health screenings, and food aid.

Our Impacted Communities

Each community carries a unique story, but the goal is always the same: bring hope, bring tools, and bring change.

1. Domeabra (2015)

A total of 250 souls were won,a major highlight was the conversion of the community's chief priestess and her assistant, both long-time devotees of ancestral gods, who denounced their idols and became active members of the church

2. Tomefa (2016)

The mission team reached 115 souls through various methods, including crusades and counseling.

3. Danchyira (2017)

The team achieved a total of 135 souls won, utilizing dawn broadcasting in addition to traditional outreach.

4. Avornyor (2018)

A total of 219 souls were won, with a significant portion (55) coming from crusade efforts.

5. Hobor (2019)

This mission resulted in 240 souls won, nearly half of which (112) were reached through crusades.

6. Asensohu Shed (2021)

The team reached 150 souls during this outreach.

7. Nyakrom (2022)

This community saw the highest number of souls won in the decade, totaling 519.

8. Navrongo (2023)

A total of 343 souls were won. A landmark achievement was the miraculous healing of an elderly woman who suffered from severe physical pain and mobility issues; after being healed during a prayer session, she was baptized and committed her life to Christ.

9. Woe (2024)

The mission resulted in 503 souls won, with a strong emphasis on house-to-house (351) and crusade (115) outreach.

What’s Next?

The work continues. More hands. More hearts. More ground to cover. Our goal is to double our reach in the next year, focusing on sanitation, education, and sustainable community development.
