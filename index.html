Winning Souls
and Advancing the Kingdom Across the Nations

Our Mission in Action

See How Lives Are Being Transformed Across the Nations

community impact

Lasting Impact
and Transforming lives

Over the past 10 years of active missions, Zoe has witnessed the faithful hand of God at work. Through relentless outreach and dedication, we have established nine local churches across various communities — each one a beacon of hope and a center for spreading the Gospel. These local assemblies now serve as vibrant hubs of worship, discipleship, and community transformation, continuing the mission to win souls and build the Kingdom of God.

medicals service

Caring for Bodies
and Ministering to Souls in the Name of Jesus

As part of our outreach efforts, Zoe Tirzah provides essential medical services during our missions. We believe that sharing the love of Christ goes beyond words — it is demonstrated through acts of care and compassion. Our medical team offers free health screenings, treatments, and medication to individuals and families in underserved communities, bringing both physical healing and the hope of the Gospel to those we serve.

Donations

Extending a Helping Hand
and Reflecting God’s Heart to the Needy

At Zoe Tirzah, we believe that love is most powerful when it is shown through action. As part of our outreach, we donate food, clothing, and essential supplies to families and communities in need. Each act of giving reflects the heart of Christ — meeting physical needs while opening doors for the message of hope. Through these gestures of compassion, lives are restored, hearts are encouraged, and the light of the Gospel continues to shine.

Meet Our Mission Team

Dedicated men and women leading the charge for Christ across the globe.

Rev. John Doe

Mission Director

Mission Director

The Mission Director oversees and coordinates all outreach and charitable activities within the church. They are responsible for planning and executing programs that empower communities, provide essential resources, and support the spread of the gospel. Their role includes organizing volunteers, managing donations, liaising with community partners, and ensuring that every initiative aligns with the church's mission and values. By leading these efforts, the Mission Director ensures that the church’s outreach is impactful, efficient, and sustainable.

Mary Agyeman

Field Coordinator

Field Coordinator

The Field Coordinator manages and oversees the implementation of outreach programs and mission activities on the ground. They work closely with volunteers, community leaders, and church staff to ensure that projects are executed efficiently and effectively. Responsibilities include organizing logistics, monitoring progress, reporting outcomes, and addressing challenges in real time. By coordinating field operations, the Field Coordinator ensures that the church’s mission initiatives have a meaningful and sustainable impact in the communities they serve.

Daniel Mensah

Community Health Lead

Community Health Lead

The Community Health Lead is responsible for planning, implementing, and overseeing health and wellness initiatives within the community. This role involves assessing community health needs, coordinating health programs, educating residents on preventive care, and collaborating with volunteers and local health partners. By leading these efforts, the Community Health Lead ensures that community members have access to essential health services, resources, and education, ultimately promoting healthier and more resilient communities.
