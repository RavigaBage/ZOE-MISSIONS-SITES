Why Axim?

A Historic City
Calling for the Gospel

Axim is one of Ghana's oldest towns, nestled on the beautiful Western coast. Despite its rich history, many communities here remain unreached by the full power of the Gospel — families who need healing, children who need hope, and hearts that are open to transformation.

Zoe Tirzah sees Axim as a strategic gateway — a city where planting deep roots for Christ can ripple out across the Western Region and beyond.

Coastal city, W. Region
Underserved communities
High youth population
Limited healthcare access
Mixed religious landscape
What We'll Do

Four Pillars of the Axim Mission

Every activity is a deliberate act of love — meeting people where they are, spirit, soul, and body.

Gospel Evangelism

Open-air crusades, door-to-door outreach, and church services bringing the transforming message of Christ to every home in Axim.

Medical Outreach

Free health screenings, treatments, and medications provided by our volunteer medical team to underserved families and communities.

Community Giving

Distributing donated clothes, food, and essential supplies to families in need — practical love that opens hearts to the Gospel.

Children's Ministry

Dedicated children's programmes with music, Bible stories, activities, and teaching — nurturing the next generation of disciples.
